FAQ

Common Questions

How long is a consultation?
A typical consultation lasts 15–30 minutes depending on the complexity of your concern. Follow-up consultations are usually shorter, around 10–15 minutes. You can always extend if needed.
Can I get a prescription?
Yes, our doctors can issue digital prescriptions after your consultation. These are valid at all major pharmacies across India and are sent to your registered email and app instantly.
What if I need a follow-up?
Follow-up consultations within 7 days are free with the same doctor. Simply go to "My Care" and rebook under the same consultation thread. Your doctor will have full context from the previous visit.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We are ABDM registered and NABH certified. All data is encrypted end-to-end and stored on ISO-certified servers in India. We follow strict HIPAA-equivalent privacy standards.
Can I cancel or reschedule?
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before your appointment for a full refund. Changes can be made from the "My Care" section of your account. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.
