Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Are nurses certified?

Yes, all our nurses hold valid nursing certificates (GNM/BSc Nursing) and are registered with the Indian Nursing Council. They undergo additional training specific to home care before joining our platform.

What areas do you cover?

We currently serve 100+ cities across India including all major metros — Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, and Ahmedabad. We're expanding to more cities every month.

Can I choose my nurse?

We match you with the best-suited nurse based on your requirements. If you've had a positive experience with a specific nurse, you can request them for future bookings subject to availability.

What about emergencies?

Our 24/7 support helpline is always available. In case of a medical emergency, our nurses are trained to provide first response and will immediately coordinate with nearby hospitals and your consulting doctor.
