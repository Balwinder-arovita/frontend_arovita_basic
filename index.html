How Arovita Works

Healthcare is not a one-time visit. It's a continuous journey.

Arovita connects every step of care — from consultation to follow-ups — creating an ongoing healthcare loop instead of isolated visits.

1
Book Consultation
User schedules in-clinic or tele-consultation.
2
Visit Doctor / Tele-Consult
Doctor evaluates symptoms and suggests next steps.
3
Get Prescription
Medication, tests, or care plan recommended.
4
Book Lab Tests
Diagnostics booked directly through Arovita.
5
Receive Care
Treatment, home care, or medication support delivered.
6
Follow-up Triggered
System reminds for review, repeat tests, or monitoring.
7
Continuous Monitoring
Vitals, records, and progress tracked over time.
8
Next Consultation
Data flows back to doctor for ongoing care. Then it loops again.
